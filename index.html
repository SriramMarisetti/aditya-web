Aditya Degree College is part of the Aditya Educational Institutions, a group of academic institutions in Andhra Pradesh, India. It offers undergraduate programs in various disciplines like science, commerce, and arts.The college is known for its emphasis on providing quality education with modern facilities,experienced faculty,and a curriculum that blends academic knowledge with practical skills.In addition to regular degree programs such as B.Sc, B.Com, and B.A., Aditya Degree College often provides training and workshops aimed at enhancing employability, including soft skills development, industry-relevant certifications, and placement assistance. The institution also encourages extracurricular activities, fostering overall student growth in both academics and personal development.

At Aditya college the entire teaching-learning process is concentrated on preparing students to make meaningful contributions to society as engaged citizens and leaders in a complex world.
